Research

My primary research is focused on X-ray binaries, where a compact object such as a neutron star or black hole accretes material from a nearby companion star. For my PhD thesis, I am applying a higher order time series analysis technique called the bispectrum to Quasi Periodic Oscillations (QPOs) from black hole binaries. By using this new (to astronomy!) technique, we can learn a lot about the geometry of the regions close to the black hole where the high energy X-rays originate.
